The punisher, alongside One Shot, are my main projects as of right now. I've attempted FPS rigs before, but I'll be honest, I was never able to get down the various intricacies of creating viewmodels for Source 1. For this rig, I have a simpler gun, better tools, and way better hands to work with. This gun, if you're unfamiliar, is from Resident Evil 4 (2023). I plan on porting more guns/props from RE4, and making content for gamemode devs to use in their projects. As for my gamemode, One Shot, it is mostly a test project for me to try out the S&Box workflow. So far, S&Box has been really fun to work with. I can only imagine what people will create. I have no doubt that S&Box can easily hit the top 10 if they play their cards right. Developers are already showing off their talent even while the API is changing almost daily. I'm excited for the future. :)
